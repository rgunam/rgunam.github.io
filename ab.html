About

SHORT SEMBLANCE
I received the BSc degree on Computer Engineering on 2013, and the MSc degree on Electrical Egineering (Instrumentation) on 2015. I am currently a PhD Candidate on Electrical Engineering. All degrees are from Universidad Nacional Autónoma de México. In 2013 I became a professor assistant up to 2015 at Facultad de Ingeniería, UNAM. Afterwards, I became a part time lecturer at FI, position that I currently hold. The given lectures are about electric and electronics topics as well as applied sciences.
My research interests are focused on:

  • Instrumentation and sensors
  • Embedded Systems
  • VLSI Design
  • System Identification
  • Signal Processing
  • Automatic Control
  • Artificial Inteligence Algorithms
